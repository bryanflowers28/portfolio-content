Today the Pistons are in a rebuilding phase, one of the few times in the franchises history where this is the case. In recent memory the Pistons legacy has been nothing more than first round playoff exits and dissapointing draft picks. From missing on superstars in the draft to regularly getting blown out by the better squads in the east, and this season has been much of the same

But the Pistons were up front about what this season would entail from the moment they introduced Troy Weaver as general manager last June and began improving their young core. In a stroke of luck, the Pistons were granted the #1 overall pick in the 2021 draft. This blessing was used to draft superstar young talent Cade Cunningham. Paired with other young stars such as Sadiq Bey and Isaiah Steward, perhaps the Pistons are getting back on the right track.
